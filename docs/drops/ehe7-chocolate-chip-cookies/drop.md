1. Preheat the oven to 350°F or 375°F and place the baking rack in the middle.
2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt; set aside.
3. In a separate bowl, cream margarine and sugar until light and fluffy.
4. Add eggs and vanilla to the creamed mixture and beat well.
5. Gradually blend in the flour mixture to the creamed mixture and mix well.
6. Stir in the chocolate chips; make sure they are evenly distributed.
7. Drop by teaspoonfuls or tablespoonfuls onto an ungreased cookie sheet, about 1 inch apart.
8. Bake for 8 to 12 minutes until the cookies are slightly underdone and the centers are just set and a light golden brown.
9. Allow the cookies to cool on the cookie sheet for 1 minute before removing them to cooling racks until they are completely cooled.
